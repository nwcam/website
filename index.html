Cami Peterson

Hello! My name is Cami Peterson and I'm a singer currently studying at the California Institute of the Arts (or Calarts for short). I like to think of my work and my inspirations as very eclectic. I mostly focus on performance rather than writing, my inspirations being 70s performers such as Chaka Kahn, Gladys Knight, and Manhattan Transfer. I also like to perform classical music, most of which is mid-20th century coloratura soprano arias and art songs, focusing mainly on correct technique meshed with emotion and connection. I also write music, inspired by topics such as mental health, and current issues like intersectional feminism, racism, sexism, climate change, and LGBTQ issues. My orchestral music is largely inspired from the music within the Legend of Zelda games. Currently, my goals for the future include writing more, releasing an EP, forming a band, performing more in general, conquering my classical objectives, and to produce and create electronic music as well. Hope you enjoy!
